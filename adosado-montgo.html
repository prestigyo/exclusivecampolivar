The home

A house built for everyday life

A three-storey townhouse on one of Godella's quiet streets, within the Campolivar residential area. The layout splits the space sensibly: a bedroom on the ground floor for anyone who would rather not climb stairs, and a generous bedroom on the lower level with an exterior window — genuinely bright, not an interior room.

Two full bathrooms and a separate WC keep the morning rush easy. The community adds what photos do not always show: a pool for the summer and a children's playground at street level, so the little ones can play outdoors without crossing the city.

  • Ground-floor bedroom, ideal for guests or flexible use
  • Main bedroom on the lower level with an exterior window
  • 2 full bathrooms + separate WC
  • Community pool for residents only
  • Children's playground within the complex
  • Minutes from central Valencia and the CV-35
