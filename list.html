Who Visits

An audience, not a crowd

The people who find Exclusive Campolivar are not browsing the whole market. They are researching one neighbourhood — Campolivar — often from abroad, often in English, and usually with a clear intention to buy.

They tend to be relocating professionals, families and investors who already understand what an established, low-density address is worth. Presenting your home here means reaching the buyers most likely to value it, without the noise of a mass portal.

Pricing

Flexible, and clear from the start

Our model is deliberately flexible. Depending on whether there is a sales agreement in place, a listing can be offered free of charge or on a commission basis — agreed openly before anything goes live.

There are no hidden fees and no long tie-ins. We would rather earn the relationship than lock it in. Tell us about your property and we will propose terms that fit.

  • Free listing where no sales agreement is in place
  • Commission-based when we handle the sale
  • Terms agreed transparently, up front
  • No hidden fees, no long tie-ins
  • A small, curated roster — your home is never lost in a list
