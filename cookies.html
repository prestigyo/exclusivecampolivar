Cookie Policy

Last updated: 16 June 2026

This policy explains what cookies and similar technologies exclusivecampolivar.com uses, and how you can control them. We use the minimum necessary and never load analytics until you have given consent.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small files a website stores in your browser. Similar technologies (such as your browser's local storage) work the same way. They let a site remember choices or measure how it is used.

2. Cookies we use

Strictly necessary — your cookie choice

To remember whether you accepted or declined analytics, we store a single value (ah-cookie-consent) in your browser's local storage. This is required for the Site to respect your decision and is exempt from consent. It contains no personal data and is not shared.

Analytics — only with your consent

If you click "Accept" in the cookie banner, we load Google Analytics 4 (provider: Google Ireland Ltd.), which sets cookies such as _ga and _ga_<id> to measure visits in a pseudonymised way. These typically expire after up to 24 months. If you decline, these cookies are never set.

  • Purpose: understand which pages are visited so we can improve the Site.
  • Provider: Google — see Google's privacy policy.

We do not use advertising or profiling cookies.

3. Manage or change your choice

You can change your decision at any time:

This reopens the banner so you can accept or decline again. You can also delete cookies and local storage through your browser settings, and configure your browser to block them.

4. Changes

We may update this policy if the cookies we use change. The "last updated" date above always reflects the current version.
